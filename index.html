Privacy Policy for Habit Bridge

Data Collection

Habit Bridge stores your habit data locally on your device using Chrome's storage API. No personal data is sent to external servers.

Data Usage

Your habit configurations, progress tracking, and settings remain private and sync only through your Chrome account.

Permissions

We only request permissions necessary for core functionality: storage for data persistence, tabs for website triggers, alarms for timers, and activeTab for URL detection.
